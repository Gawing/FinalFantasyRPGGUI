			Magic and Item Help





Magic Use


-To use a magic select a magic from the magic menu and press select magic 

-You cannot use the magic if you have insufficient MP 



Magic Information


-Fire, Blizzard and Thunder  deals 2x your characters magic strength 

-Holy deals 5x your characters magic strength

-Ultima deals 20x your characters magic strength!! 



Item Use 


-To use an item, select an item in the item menu and press select item 

-You cannot use an item if you don’t have that Item




Item Information 


-Potion  heals your character at a total of 200 HP 

-Magic Stone unleashes a random elemental magic dealing 2x your character’s magic strength

- Ultima Stone deals half of the power of the Ultima Magic which is 10x your magic strength
