			Character Information 




Character Attributes 


HP = Health Points = Your characters health

MP = Mana Points  = Your character will need mana points to use magic 

Strength = Your characters attack damage 

Magic = Your characters magic strength the magic damage will depend on your magic 

Level = Your characters level and also the level of monsters 

Next Level = Experience left to gain a level 



Character Type and Abilities Information


Warrior = Has high initial HP and Strength

Marksman = Has high initial Strength and Magic

Spellcaster = Has high initial MP and Magic 




Overdrive Mode Effects

Warrior = Strength will increase by a multiply of 4

Marksman =Strength and Magic will increase by a multiply of 2 

Spellcaster = MP cost for all magic is divided by 5
